Public health key products.

Undertaker

Chlorpyrifos works by inhibiting acetyl cholinesterase (AChE), which is an enzyme that degrades neurotransmitters.On concrete slabs under construction, treat the outside of the foundation wall trenches with 6 – 12 litres of the solution per linear meter and to the depths of 40 -75 cm. replace the soil and apply another 8 – 16 litres of the solution per linear meter and cover the trench and fill the treated soil with a thin layer of untreated soil. On mud floor houses under construction, treat the external surfaces of the poles’ holes with the solution to the depth of 40 – 75 cm. Dip the poles into the solution. Treat as for building under construction. On Termites nests, remove the top 15 cm of the soil and pour the solution down the tunnels.
